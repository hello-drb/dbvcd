About

I'm a creative director and strategist with a background in fine art, digital design, and technology. My approach combines philosophical rigor with pragmatic execution—I believe the best creative work comes from truly understanding what something should be before deciding how it looks.

For the past decade, I've helped startups, nonprofits, and established organizations clarify their identity and build creative systems that scale. I specialize in brand strategy, digital product design, and content architecture.

Currently, I'm exploring how AI can enhance creative thinking without replacing human judgment. I'm interested in building tools that help teams make better decisions faster.

I'm based in Brooklyn, NY. When I'm not designing, I'm thinking about complexity, teaching workshops on brand strategy, or writing about the intersection of philosophy and design.
